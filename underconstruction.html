Untitled (Under Construction)

In-situ installation, 2023, collaboration projet with Yuzhe Wang and Inès Aourdache, inkjet-printed photos, metalic stand, rocks, wool threads, variable dimension

This installation work is the continuation of the Sans titre (Yuto Fuwara) project. It is presented on the occasion of the exhibition Des Origins at the Art School of Belfort in 2023.
