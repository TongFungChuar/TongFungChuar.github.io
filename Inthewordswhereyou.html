In The Words Where You've Touched Are Soft

Dalam Kata-kata Di Mana Kau Sentuh Lembut
Video Installation, 10min5sec, 2024

This video comprises erratic dreamscape scenes where viewers are invited to integrate into the fictional narration. The empty scenes of the basement's humidity and tropical forest are accompanied by a soft woman's voice gently speaking in Malay. In the installation, images are modified with a constant flashlight and surreal lighting, creating an additional layer of sensation. They fortify the nonexistence while emphasising a harsh ugliness common in third-world countries. The monologue suggests a story of foreign experience with war scenes and a hysterical mental state, intending to transcribe the reception and regard for the real-world major conflict continuously happening as a foreigner in these contexts. By connecting both elements, the inner contrasts of fanatical idealism and those uninfected are revealed, as if the dilemma has turned into a sado-masochistic dream of pain fulfilment.
