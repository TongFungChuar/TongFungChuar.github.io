The Dream of Being the Prophetic Fever

Ongoing drawing series (2023 - )

Growing up, I was disturbed by a form that I could not grasp. It can be linked to the uniformity in the patriarchal society or the trauma to the impotence of the family system or something else outside of my feelings...this form today always remains a mystery to me. This is why I am making this series of drawings. By interpreting it in a lyrical and metaphorical way, I seek to create a therapeutic experience towards this questioning.
